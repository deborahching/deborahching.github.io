方日 ROOM

Exhibition design worked by a group of 5 to explore the relationship among people and bedrooms.

Bedroom is a personal space where it can reveal a lot of privacy, personal choices, habits, and present the way of living of the owner.「方日」comes from the two Chinese characters inside the word 「房間」(bedroom).

“Personal space” is the theme of our exhibition. A personal space provides us the experience and freedom of doing whatever we want. In the exhibition, we recreate this experience by bringing the private space (i.e. bedroom) into public.

Space 1

Privacy | Permission | Character
In this space, visitors get to view the various items present in a bedroom (from miscellaneous to private items) in different levels of visual clarity as decided by the owner, presenting the experience of privacy, permission, and character.
