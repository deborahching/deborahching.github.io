Eco-House

Climate change, has been greatly intensify in recent years, the issue had been threatening the living of human beings and especially our next generation. This project is a collaboration with the Rentemestervej's Library, to raise the awareness of the public on the issue. THE ECO-HOUSE OF 2050 is the concept created, which is a modern house filled with recycled and sophisticated furniture. During the exhibition 5 posters that represents eco-house’s artefacts, and 5 audio sounds and descriptions to immerge the spectator in the eco-house will be displayed. 

The poster is showcasing what recycled materials are used to create the artefacts. By seeing the artefacts through the materials they are built from, a clear suggestion could be give to audience and allow them to use their own imagination on this proposed future.

Exhibition setup

The exhibition is planned to simulate a home feeling, audience are requires to walk through "doors" to see different artefacts which to feel like walking from rooms to rooms at home. Also, 10-15 sec audio of the artefacts will be provided, which allows audience to have better imagination of the future world.
