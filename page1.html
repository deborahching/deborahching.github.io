John Doughnuts

This is a project for a doughnuts brand ready to start a business, John Doughnuts, in Copenhagen. In order to help them start, a visual identity was created for their brand, based on their company tenet, bringing joy to the office and sustainable.

Logo Design

With the company tent, bring joy to others, an enjoy facial expression and the doughnuts was the key elements of the logo, presenting the joyful concept. 

Brand Pattern

Brand pattern and brand colour scheme was created based on the toppings colours of different doughnuts flavour, extracting the colours and shapes of the ingredients.
The colours of the brand was more organic and natural, which echos with their company tent, creating more healthy snacks.

Packaging Design

Inside the packaging, an empty space allow customer to leave their words to their colleagues.
After grabbing up the doughnuts, some fun facts of the doughnuts ingredients will be shown.

Social Media Strategy

Delivery Van Design

Another service the company aimed to provide is the delivery service, as they are a startup company, promotion of brand is important, a delivery van was also designed to further advertise the company.
