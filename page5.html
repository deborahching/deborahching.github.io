Experimental Book Design

An experimental book design of a Japanese mythical oni, Shuten-dōji 酒吞童子. The book concept is based on the two appearances of Shuten-dōji, handsome boy and monster. Which a neat and clean cover was the first cover simulating the handsome boy, while after opening the book a catchy bloody red and a dark cover come into view, simulating the monster appearance.
